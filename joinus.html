Junior Scientist – Molecular Biology / Microbiology

We are seeking a highly motivated Junior Scientist with hands-on experience in molecular biology and microbiology. The successful candidate will contribute to experimental activities supporting enzyme discovery, protein engineering, and biotechnology research programs.

Desired Qualifications

  • M.Sc. or B.Tech./M.Tech. in Biotechnology, Microbiology, Molecular Biology, Life Sciences, or related disciplines.
  • Hands-on experience in microbial culture and strain handling.
  • Experience with routine molecular biology techniques including PCR, cloning, gel electrophoresis, and DNA analysis.
  • Experience with recombinant protein expression is desirable.
  • Strong experimental record-keeping and documentation skills.
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a multidisciplinary environment.

What You Will Work On

  • Microbial strain development and maintenance.
  • Molecular cloning and construct generation.
  • Protein expression and characterization.
  • Supporting enzyme engineering and biotechnology research programs.
  • Contributing to the development of next-generation industrial biocatalysts.
